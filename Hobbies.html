Travel

With its beauty and variety, our world can put any artwork to shame, and one of my dreams was to be able to see all of it... The fact that I can't actually do it, is one of my deepest regrets.
I categorize my vecations into 2 categories: Travel and Relaxation.
# Travel- Is when my goal is to see and do all that I can. I need to walk, climb or travel to as many places as I can and do as much as I can in the given time of the trip.
# Relaxation- Is when I simply want to take my time and enjoy myself. Here I don't prioritise the "Do as much as I can" attitude, and focus more on "what I'd like to do better".
