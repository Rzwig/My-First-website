About me

Hello! I am Ran Zwighaft. As a kid, I used to be a shy with a lot of self-doubts, but with time and the help of my family and friends I realize my own value and I learn that if I don't give up and put my all into something, I can achieve almost anything.

I am a man who love to dream, and I love fantasy. I love reading fantasy books, read manga and manhwa, watch movies, anime and mostly love playing games. I also like to travel (especially in nature), Krav Maga, and my wife.

Travel

With its beauty and variety, our world can put any artwork to shame, and one of my dreams was to be able to see all of it... The fact that I can't actually do it, is one of my deepest regrets.
I categorize my vecations into 2 categories: Travel and Relaxation.
# Travel is when my goal is to see all that I can, so I need to walk, climb or travel to as many places as I can and do as much as I can.
# Relaxation is to simply take my time and enjoy myself. Here I don't prioritise the "Do as much as I can" attitude, and focus more on "what I'd like to do better".
